1. Academic papers on adaptive interfaces
2. AI transparency frameworks
3. Behavioral design studies
4. Carnegie Mellon privacy research

## Key Insight

The common thread across authoritative sources: sustainable engagement and lasting trust require transparency, user control, and ethical design thinking. Resources emphasizing manipulation consistently underperform those teaching genuine value creation.
